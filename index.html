About Us

Explore Entrepreneurship Cell, Sharda University, your ultimate launchpad for innovation. Immerse yourself in dynamic workshops, thought-provoking speaker sessions, and high-stakes competitions to fuel your entrepreneurial journey. With personalized guidance, crucial funding opportunities, and a robust network, we democratize entrepreneurship within our vibrant ecosystem. Whether you're an aspiring enthusiast or a seasoned entrepreneur, dive into our diverse array of events, including Conclaves, Expert Talks, Industry-defined Problems, Campaigns, Pitching Sessions, and Peer-to-Peer Interactions. Embrace personal and collaborative growth with us at Sharda University's E-Cell. Join us and unlock your entrepreneurial potential today.

Our Aim:

Our Entrepreneurship Cell is your launchpad for success. We offer resources, support, and networking opportunities, promoting entrepreneurship as a viable career. Join us to cultivate innovation, access funding, and turn your ideas into thriving ventures!

Our Vision:

Cultivates a dynamic startup culture, championing innovative ideas among students. We empower individuals with skills and support to transform ideas into thriving businesses, fostering innovation through mentorship, networking, and funding opportunities. Join us to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs and drive positive societal impact.

Our Mission:

We will serve as a cornerstone for aspiring student entrepreneurs, offering essential support and guidance. With a focus on fostering an entrepreneurial culture, we will prepare students for real-world challenges, equipping them with skills through mentorship and practical experiences. Our aim is to cultivate an entrepreneurial spirit among students, exposing them to India's thriving startup ecosystem and inspiring them to embark on their entrepreneurial journeys.

Our Motto:

Ideation:

Embark on entrepreneurial ideation at Sharda University's E-cell. We merge creativity and problem-solving, crafting innovative concepts that meet market needs. Thrive on exploration, experimentation, and collaboration as you refine ideas iteratively for execution.

Execution:

Experience the transformation from idea to reality at Sharda University's E-cell. Precision, resource allocation, and strategic implementation drive tangible outcomes. Adaptability, resilience, and attention to detail guide entrepreneurs through complexities. It's a journey of continuous learning, monitoring, and the realization of impactful visions.
